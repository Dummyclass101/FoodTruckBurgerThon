Our Story


      

How Did BurgerThon come to be?

Once upon a time, we found ourselves yearning to
bring our love for food and conversation to the
streets. With a desire to connect with people on a
more personal level, we decided to embark on a delicious
adventure by starting my very own burger food truck.

With each burger served, we strived to push the boundaries of flavor
and foster a sense of community. we sourced ingredients from local
farmers and artisans, ensuring the freshest and most sustainable
options for my customers. By engaging in conversations about food
origins, sustainability, and culinary creativity, we encouraged a
deeper appreciation for the art of cooking and the joy of savoring a
well-crafted burger.

Today, the BurgerThon food truck continues to roam the streets,
bringing together people from all walks of life around
a shared love for great food and meaningful conversations.
As a team with a passion for culinary exploration, we
are grateful for the opportunity to create unforgettable
burger experiences and forge connections with every customer
who steps up to our window.
